## NFT Basics

### What is an NFT?
- Unique digital asset on a blockchain
- Represents ownership of digital content
- Transferable and tradeable
- Recorded on public blockchain

## Creating NFT Listings

### Eligibility
- Must be verified creator
- Must own rights to content
- Content must comply with platform policies

### Minting Process
- Upload digital content
- Set starting bid and auction parameters
- Pay gas fees for minting
- NFT created on blockchain

### Content Requirements
- Original content or proper licensing
- High-quality digital files
- Appropriate metadata
- Clear ownership proof

## Auction Process

### Starting an Auction
- Set starting bid amount
- Set auction duration
- Set reserve price (optional)
- Auction becomes active upon submission

### Bidding
- Bids are binding commitments
- Minimum bid increments apply
- Bid cannot be withdrawn
- Highest bid wins at auction end

### Auction Completion
- Winner notified automatically
- Payment processed immediately
- NFT transferred to winner's wallet
- Seller receives payment minus fees

## Smart Contracts

### Binding Terms
- Smart contract terms are automatic and binding
- Transactions cannot be reversed
- Code execution is final
- Blockchain record is authoritative

### Gas Fees
- Buyer pays blockchain gas fees
- Gas fees separate from purchase price
- Gas fees non-refundable
- Fees vary based on network congestion

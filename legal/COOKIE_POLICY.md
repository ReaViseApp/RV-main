# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
**Purpose**: Required for the platform to function
**Duration**: Session or 1 year
**Examples**:
- Authentication tokens
- Session management
- Security features

**Can be disabled**: No (platform won't work properly)

### Functional Cookies
**Purpose**: Remember your preferences
**Duration**: Up to 1 year
**Examples**:
- Language preferences
- Display settings
- Theme preferences

**Can be disabled**: Yes (may impact experience)

### Analytics Cookies
**Purpose**: Understand how you use our platform
**Duration**: Up to 2 years
**Examples**:
- Page views
- Navigation paths
- Feature usage
- Error tracking

**Can be disabled**: Yes

### Advertising Cookies
**Purpose**: Show relevant advertisements
**Duration**: Up to 1 year
**Examples**:
- Ad targeting
- Campaign measurement
- Third-party ads

**Can be disabled**: Yes

### Social Media Cookies
**Purpose**: Enable social media features
**Duration**: Varies by provider
**Examples**:
- Social sharing
- Social login
- Embedded content

**Can be disabled**: Yes

## Cookie Management

### Browser Controls
You can control cookies through your browser:
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Delete cookies
- Set preferences per site

### Our Cookie Preferences
Visit Settings > Privacy to manage cookie preferences.

### Impact of Disabling
- Essential: Platform may not work
- Functional: Settings won't be remembered
- Analytics: We can't improve the service
- Advertising: Ads will be less relevant

## Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track signals where technically feasible.

Manage through device settings.

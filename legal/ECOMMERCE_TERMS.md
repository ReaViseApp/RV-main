# E-Commerce Purchase Terms

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## General Purchase Terms

These E-Commerce Purchase Terms apply to all transactions on the ReaVise platform.

## Order Process

### Making a Purchase
1. Add items to your cart using the "Curate" button
2. Review items in your Curated page
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Provide required contact and shipping information
5. Select payment method
6. Agree to all required terms
7. Complete purchase

### Order Confirmation
- You will receive an order confirmation email
- Confirmation does not guarantee acceptance
- Sellers have the right to accept or decline orders

## Pricing and Payment

### Pricing
- All prices in USD unless otherwise stated
- Prices may change without notice
- Price at time of purchase applies
- Additional taxes may apply based on location

### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards via Stripe
- PayPal
- Payment information is securely processed
- We do not store full payment details

### Payment Authorization
By completing a purchase, you authorize us to charge your payment method for the total amount.

## Transaction Categories

### The Lot Purchases
- Purchasing customization services for your materials
- Buyer provides materials to seller
- Terms negotiated between buyer and seller

### Design Purchases
- Purchasing creative designs and ideas
- Digital or physical delivery as specified
- License terms apply to digital content

### ReaVise Purchases
- Purchasing completed products
- Includes verified reviews and quality assurance
- Shipping terms as specified by seller

## Seller Responsibilities

Sellers must:
- Accurately describe products/services
- Fulfill orders in a timely manner
- Communicate with buyers
- Provide tracking information when applicable
- Honor their stated policies

## Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers must:
- Provide accurate information
- Pay for completed transactions
- Communicate with sellers
- Review and rate completed transactions

## Shipping and Delivery

### Shipping
- Shipping costs displayed at checkout
- Delivery times are estimates
- International shipping may incur customs fees
- Buyer responsible for customs fees

### Digital Delivery
- Digital products delivered electronically
- Access granted upon payment confirmation
- No refunds for digital products after access

## Returns and Refunds

### Return Policy
- Return policies set by individual sellers
- Review seller's policy before purchase
- Return window typically 14-30 days
- Items must be in original condition

### Refund Process
- Refunds processed within 5-10 business days
- Refunded to original payment method
- Shipping costs typically non-refundable
- Partial refunds may apply

### Disputes
- Contact seller first to resolve issues
- Platform mediation available
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension

## Cancellations

### By Buyer
- Cancel before seller accepts order
- Cancellation after acceptance subject to seller policy
- Full refund for cancelled orders

### By Seller
- Seller may cancel if unable to fulfill
- Full refund issued for seller cancellations
- Communication required for cancellations

## Intellectual Property Rights

### Copyright
All content is protected by copyright law:
- Original works automatically protected
- Registration not required but recommended
- Duration varies by jurisdiction
- Protection is automatic upon creation

### Trademarks
- Protect brand names and logos
- Require registration in most jurisdictions
- Unauthorized use prohibited
- Fair use exceptions apply

### Trade Secrets
- Protect confidential business information
- Requires reasonable secrecy measures
- No registration required
- Lost if publicly disclosed

### Patents
- Protect inventions and processes
- Require formal registration
- Public disclosure required
- Limited duration (typically 20 years)

## DMCA Compliance

### Copyright Infringement Reporting

If you believe your copyright has been infringed:

**1. Submit DMCA Notice to:**
- Email: copyright@reavise.com
- Address: [Your DMCA Agent Address]

**2. Include:**
- Your contact information
- Description of copyrighted work
- Location of infringing content (URL)
- Statement of good faith belief
- Statement under penalty of perjury
- Physical or electronic signature

**3. We Will:**
- Review notice promptly
- Remove or disable infringing content
- Notify the uploader
- Implement repeat infringer policy

### Counter-Notice

If your content was removed and you believe it was a mistake:

**1. Submit Counter-Notice with:**
- Your contact information
- Identification of removed content
- Statement under penalty of perjury
- Consent to jurisdiction
- Physical or electronic signature

**2. Process:**
- We forward to complainant
- Content may be restored in 10-14 days
- Unless complainant files lawsuit

### Repeat Infringer Policy
- Three strikes policy
- Account termination for repeat infringers
- Appeals process available

## Fair Use and Exceptions

### Fair Use Doctrine
Limited use without permission for:
- Commentary and criticism
- News reporting
- Teaching and education
- Research
- Parody

Determined by:
- Purpose and character of use
- Nature of copyrighted work
- Amount used
- Effect on market value

### Platform Position
- We respect fair use rights
- Consider fair use in DMCA responses
- Users responsible for fair use claims
- Legal determination if disputed

## Licensing Your Content

### License Types

**All Rights Reserved**
- Default copyright protection
- Permission required for any use
- Most restrictive

**Creative Commons**
- Various license options
- Standardized terms
- Easy to understand
- Globally recognized

**Royalty-Free**
- One-time payment
- Unlimited use within terms
- Not necessarily free
- Terms specified by licensor

**Rights Managed**
- Specific use parameters
- Based on usage type, duration, geography
- Typically higher cost
- More control for creator

### Setting Terms
When posting, you can:
- Specify license type
- Set usage restrictions
- Allow/prohibit commercial use
- Allow/prohibit derivative works
- Set attribution requirements
